Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

Stardew Companion is a fan-made reference app for the video game Stardew Valley. This policy explains what data the app collects and how it is used.

Data We Collect

Stardew Companion does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. The app has no accounts, no login, and no connection to any server.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app saves your progress locally on your device using standard browser storage (localStorage). This includes:

  • Community Center bundle completion checkboxes
  • Museum donation checkboxes
  • Cooking recipe learned/unlearned status

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party. You can clear it at any time by uninstalling the app.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use any analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or third-party SDKs that collect data.

Children's Privacy

The app does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date at the top.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the GitHub repository.
